Section 1Headline snapshot

In one paragraph: Livermore is a working retail-quant strategy platform. The "Use a Template" flow runs end-to-end; users can browse, customize risk, backtest, save, publish, and receive signal alerts. The "Custom Build" flow has the scaffolding but the signal-composition layer is missing — meaning users currently tune templates rather than truly build custom strategies. Two small investments (signal composer, asset behavior fingerprint) close the largest gaps.

ShippedProduction code in use
PartialExists but limited or stub
MissingNot built yet

What's true today

Strength
22 strategy types, 4 signal providers
Engine handles trend, momentum, mean-reversion, factor, event-driven, sentiment, multi-factor composites. Disclosure-date lag in fundamental providers prevents lookahead.
Strength
Full robustness suite
Parameter sensitivity grids, sub-period analysis, transaction-cost sensitivity, peer-ticker comparison. Most retail platforms charge for this.
Strength
Live signal computation + alerts
Cron recomputes signal per saved strategy; email dispatched on signal change. Closes the "tell me when to act" loop.

What's not done

Gap
Signal composition UI
"Custom Build" currently shows read-only WHEN IN / WHEN OUT — users can't combine primitives (e.g. "above 200-day MA AND RSI > 50").
Gap
Asset behavior fingerprint
No "this stock has trended 65% of the time" check at picker time. Users can apply mean-reversion templates to strongly trending stocks with no warning.
Gap
Survivorship-bias-free backtests
Cross-sectional templates use current S&P 500 membership for historical backtests. Overstates returns ~1-3% per year.

Section 4Top 6 gaps to close

Ranked by user impact ÷ engineering effort. The first three are high-impact, small-effort wins; the next three are medium-impact, medium-effort.

#1 High impact
Signal composer UI
~11 h
The missing piece that makes "Custom Build" truly custom. Today WHEN IN / WHEN OUT are read-only template-baked copy.
Build: schema field logic_with_prior, engine multi-rule fold, frontend primitive catalog, checkbox-AND/OR composer UI, StrategyJSON wiring.
#2 High impact
Asset behavior fingerprint
~3 h
Cheap; cuts the "wrong template for wrong asset" failure mode in both flows. "NVDA has trended 70% of the time — RSI mean-reversion not ideal."
Build: backend service computing {trending_pct, mean_reverting_pct, realized_vol, max_dd_5y} from price history. Surface in picker step.
#3 High impact
Cross-block consistency check
~1 h
One-hour win. Catches configuration mistakes like "trend entry + mean-reversion exit" with a soft warning.
Build: extend strategy_validator.validate_strategy with semantic mismatch flag. Returns warning, not error.
#4 Medium impact
Turnover-vs-cost realism check
~1 h
Catches "daily reversal + 5 bps slippage = unprofitable" before backtest. Cheap heuristic.
Build: compute expected_turnover × total_bps; warn if > 50% of typical expected return.
#5 Medium impact
Per-primitive fire-rate panel
~3 h
Teaches users about their strategy. "Your AND composition has primitive A firing 95% of the time — it's not really helping."
Build: engine counts per-primitive truth values per bar; surface in result panel.
#6 Medium impact
template_usage_events table
~2 h
Closes the loop for the quarterly iteration cycle's Step 1 — see which templates are hot / cold / never used.
Build: SQLAlchemy model + endpoint to log backtest_run / saved / forked / thumbs_down per template.

Section 6Where you sit on the maturity curve

Livermore is past the "retail toy" threshold and into the "credible retail-quant platform" band — well above no-tier-controls homemade backtesters, comfortably below institutional-tier platforms.

Toy backtester Retail clones Composer

Above you: composable user-defined signal primitives in a sandboxed DSL, long/short with borrow modeling, walk-forward optimization with parameter refit, paper-then-live broker integration, options strategies.

Below you (already cleared): naïve in-memory backtester with no tier controls, "backtest only" platforms with no save/share/monitor loop, platforms that show Sharpe without flagging credibility, platforms with no survivorship/lookahead defense at all, chat-AIs that make up numbers without citation.
